About Aromiq

Aromiq is a trusted supplier of premium-grade essential oils sourced directly from the fertile lands of Indonesia. We specialize in Patchouli and Nutmeg oils for export markets, serving manufacturers, distributors, and wellness brands worldwide.

Our commitment to quality begins at the source – working directly with local farmers who practice sustainable cultivation methods. Each batch of essential oil undergoes rigorous testing to ensure the highest standard of purity and potency.

With years of experience in essential oil production and export, we understand international market requirements and provide reliable solutions for your aromatic ingredient needs.
